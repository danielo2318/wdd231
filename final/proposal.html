TEPETL Trail Club

León, Guanajuato

Club Name

TEPETL (from Nahuatl "mountain")

A short, powerful name with Mexican roots that represents our passion for mountain trail running.

Suggested domain: tepetl.run

Website Purpose

  • Showcase trail running routes in León and surrounding areas
  • Publish race calendar and group training schedules
  • Provide mountain running tips
  • Promote club membership
  • Display event galleries and landscapes

User Scenarios

  1. "Where and when do you meet for runs?"
  2. "What gear do I need to start trail running?"
  3. "How can I join the club?"
  4. "Which routes do you recommend for beginners?"

Color Scheme

#FF5E5B
#333

Usage:

  • #FF5E5B: Headers, buttons, highlights
  • #333: Main text, borders, secondary elements
  • White: Background and negative space

Typography

Montserrat (700): Titles and headings

Roboto (400/500): Body text and subtitles

Modern, readable combination that works well on screens.
